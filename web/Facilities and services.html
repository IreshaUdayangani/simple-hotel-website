OUR FACILITIES


Your every convenience is catered to with a wide range of facilities and services. The unique ‘walk in’ swimming pool is extensive with a swim-up bar, Jacuzzi and a kiddie’s area. There is a fully equipped gym for fitness fanatics. Billiards and other indoor games for the more laid back and Tennis, Table Tennis, Volleyball, Squash and beach games for the restless; with a health centre to enjoy a soothing massage afterwards. Enjoy retail therapy at the shopping arcade and pamper yourself at the beauty salon, then dance the night away at the lively in-house Night club. Apart from the services such as laundry, currency exchange, concierge, parking, 24 hour reception, we are available to offer you assistance and meet your specific requests anytime. We also offer a full range of business services at our business centre, with conference and banqueting facilities too.
